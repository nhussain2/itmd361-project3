Internship at TEXT 8398 as Junior Architect

In the summer of 2017, from months July to August, I joined the TEXT team in Karachi to help in designing their new office. This office served to be Pakistan's first technology innovation center. The project was an old factory conversation project in Korangi Industrial Area. During this time, I was able to:

  • Design a to-scale 3D Sketchup model for the new office building
  • Propose alterations to the building's elevation design
  • Help design the window sections of the building
  • Create floor plans and incorporated electricals and HVAC systems to both floorplans and 3D model
  • Supervise the construction site, go for site inspections and coordinate materials with engineers, contractors and plumbers
  • Learn how to use AutoCAD in a professional work environment
  • Sketch innovative window designs to improve elevation
